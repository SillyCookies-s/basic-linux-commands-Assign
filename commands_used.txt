Linux Commands Assignment - Complete Command List

=== 1. Creating and Renaming Files/Directories ===
mkdir test_dir
touch test_dir/example.txt
mv test_dir/example.txt test_dir/renamed_example.txt
ls -la test_dir/

=== 2. Viewing File Contents ===
cat /etc/passwd
head -n 5 /etc/passwd
tail -n 5 /etc/passwd

=== 3. Searching for Patterns ===
grep "root" /etc/passwd

=== 4. Zipping and Unzipping ===
zip -r test_dir.zip test_dir/
mkdir unzipped_dir && unzip test_dir.zip -d unzipped_dir/
ls -la unzipped_dir/test_dir/

=== 5. Downloading Files ===
wget https://github.com/trinib/Linux-Bash-Commands/blob/main/README.md

=== 6. Changing Permissions ===
touch secure.txt
chmod 444 secure.txt
ls -la secure.txt

=== 7. Working with Environment Variables ===
export MY_VAR="Hello, Linux!"
echo $MY_VAR
